About

Hi, I am a front-end web developer based in Yakima, Washington. I believe in creativity and perserverance. Learning is my passion and I will forever be a student. My life has a purpose, my family is my drive and my story has just begun.

I love all things technology and have a passion for web development and design. The Last Mile Coding program taught me HTML, CSS, JavaScript and jQuery. I currently maintain five websites, four of which I built on the WordPress platform. I am a certified trusted tester through the department of Homeland Security's Office of Accessible Systems and Technology. Document remediation consumes many of my hours per week and I thoroughly enjoy creating an accessible internet for all.

I am currently enrolled in the Intro to Programming, Code the Dream program and truly appreciate this life-changing opportunity. This program is helping me expand my knowledge of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I'm excited about the future and can't wait to see what God has planned for me!
